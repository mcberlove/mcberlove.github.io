Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Empty Space Left

Ingredients

I love this cookie recipe!

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking soda
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup canola oil
  • 1/2 cup salted peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup lightly packed brown sugar
  • 3 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

Yay, Cookie Monster is excited for these cookies too! And he gets to keep them all because he would never share allergens ;)

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Farenheit.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt in a medium bowl.
  3. Beat together oil, peanut butter, and sugars on medium-high speed. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Blend in vanilla extract.
  4. Add chocolate chips to the oil mixture. Gradually beat in flour mixture. Shape into balls.
  5. Bake for 10 to 13 minutes or until golden brown.
